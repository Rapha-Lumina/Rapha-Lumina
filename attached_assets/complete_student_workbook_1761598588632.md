This workbook is your companion for the entire course. It contains:

✓ There are no "right" answers, only authentic ones  
✓ Take your time - rushing defeats the purpose  
✓ Revisit exercises as you evolve

Your consciousness is unique. Your journey is your own. Use this workbook as a tool for self-discovery, not as a test to pass.
