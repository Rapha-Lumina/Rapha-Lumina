- Mobile responsive (60%+ traffic will be mobile)

   - Compelling headline
   - Subheadline
   - Hero image/video
   - Primary CTA button
   - Trust badges (if applicable)

   - Agitate current pain
   - Show empathy
   - Build tension

3. **The Solution**
   - Introduce course
   - Show it's THE answer
   - Brief overview

4. **What You'll Learn**
   - Module breakdown
   - Specific outcomes
   - Tangible results

5. **Social Proof**
   - Testimonials
   - Number of students
   - Results/transformations
   - (Add as you get them)

6. **Your Story**
   - Brief credibility
   - Why you created this
   - Your transformation

7. **What's Included**
   - Detailed curriculum
   - All bonuses
   - Value stack

8. **Investment**
   - Price reveal
   - Payment options
   - Money-back guarantee
   - Comparison (value vs price)

9. **FAQ**
   - Address all objections
   - Remove friction

10. **Final CTA**
    - Urgency/scarcity (if genuine)
    - Last chance messaging
    - Big button

### CTA Button Best Practices:
- **Bad:** "Buy Now" / "Submit"
- **Good:** "Begin My Awakening" / "Start My Journey" / "Enrol Now"
- Colour: High contrast (gold/teal on dark background)
- Size: Large, impossible to miss
- Placement: Multiple times down the page
